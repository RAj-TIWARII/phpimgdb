Digital India

Digital India is a programme to transform India into digital empowered society and knowledge economy. The Digital India is transformational in nature and would ensure that Government services are available to citizens electronically.

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

AI promises a future where education is highly personalized, accessible, and engaging for students. AI can adapt to individual learning styles, provide tailored feedback, and even offer virtual tutoring. This can lead to deeper understanding, improved learning outcomes, and greater engagement with educational content.
